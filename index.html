About Behna

Where tradition meets contemporary elegance

Born in the vibrant city of Jaipur, Behna embodies the essence of traditional Rajasthani craftsmanship. Our story began with a passion for preserving the rich heritage of Indian textiles while creating contemporary pieces for the modern woman.

Handcrafted Excellence

Every piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who have inherited their craft through generations.

Sustainable Fashion

We believe in ethical fashion that respects both our artisans and the environment.

Timeless Design

Our designs blend traditional motifs with contemporary silhouettes for effortless elegance.
